# Description:
Intracellular messenger formed by the action of phospholipase C on phosphatidylinositol 4,5-bisphosphate, which is one of the phospholipids that make up the cell membrane. Inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate is released into the cytoplasm where it releases calcium ions from internal stores within the cell&#39;s endoplasmic reticulum. These calcium ions stimulate the activity of B kinase or calmodulin. [PubChem]
