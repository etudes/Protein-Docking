# Mechanism_Of_Action:
Methylnaltrexone is a pheriphally-acting Î¼-opioid antagonists that acts on the gastrointestinal tract inhibit opioid-induced decrease in gastric motility and transit time. Because methylnaltrexone is a quaternary derivative of naltrexone, it produces its gastrointestinal effects without producing analgesic effects or withdrawal symptoms as it does not cross the blood-brain-barrier.
