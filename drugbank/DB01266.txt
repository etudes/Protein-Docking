# Pharmacology:
Veregen is a botanical drug product for topical use. The drug substance in Veregen is Kunecatechins, which is a partially purified fraction of the water extract of green tea leaves from <em>Camellia sinensis O Kuntze</em>, and is a mixture of catechins and other green tea components. Veregen was approved by the FDA in October 2006.
